About Little Chef Bakery

Welcome to Little Chef Bakery, My name is Tyler Greco. I am a Army veteran based out of Phoenix, Arizona. My baking journey began when my wife and I wanted focus on healthier alternatives to what was provided in stores.

The name Little Chef comes from our youngest daughter always wanting to be in the kitchen while someone is cooking, calling her "Little Chef". This journey has led me to loving the art of baking and wanting to pass it down to my children.

What intrigued me was finding out that Sourdough is the oldest form of leavend bread. Sourdough can be dated to ancient Egyptians using "starter", a living culture of wild yeast and beneficial bacteria to leaven the bread naturally.

Whether you're looking for a sweet indulgence or a savory delight, Little Chef Bakery has something for everyone. Join us on this delightful journey of flavors and experience the joy of baking!
